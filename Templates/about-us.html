About Our Application

Our web application helps content creators streamline their video editing process by automatically removing silent sections from their videos. This reduces the stress of manually trimming out silent moments and ensures creators don't have to redo videos due to extended periods of silence.

The backend is stateless, meaning that once the silent sections are trimmed, the video is processed and delivered back to the user without storing any of the user's data. This ensures privacy and efficiency for content creators, allowing them to focus more on their creativity rather than editing.
